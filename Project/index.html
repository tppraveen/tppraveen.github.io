The Kraft Heinz Company

In our project, we are enhancing tomato ketchup plants worldwide for The Kraft Heinz Company. The project involves UI5 development and enhancement, along with the implementation of 12+ plants in SAP MII and SAPUI5. Our previous project utilized outdated screens and suffered from slow page loading times.


We have the task of upgrading and testing over 150+ screens within a 6-month timeframe. Our team has divided the workload and assumed leadership roles to ensure completion before the deadline. We're pleased to share that our efforts have earned appreciation from the client for our team's dedication and efficiency.




During my involvement in this project, I led end-to-end implementation, encompassing project planning, resource allocation, template design, IT infrastructure setup, deployment, rollout, go-live support, on-site assistance during go-live, and hypercare.


I specialized in developing complex pages such as inspection, Batchhub, line status monitoring, quality and maintenance notification, as well as administrative interfaces. My responsibilities encompassed building and rigorously testing intricate logic scenarios to ensure optimal functionality across these critical components.


The project focused on four key modules: Rephub, Batchhub, Weighhub, and OfflineOEE & Admin. We comprehensively addressed manufacturing execution systems, covering shift management, resource allocation, line status monitoring, downtime management, inspection, quality notification, operator menu dashboard, order initiation, maintenance alerts, OEE monitoring, weigh list management, scale integration, proposal generation, order execution, dispatch management, inquiry pages, SPC charting, plant visualization, tare weight management, and administrative tools.


During UAT and the go-live phase, I identified and resolved complex issues, playing a critical role in development. I also managed the release of orders in ECC and ensured confirmation in ECC.


This project provided valuable experience in SAPUI5, SAP MII, ECC, project management, leadership, and timely project completion.
