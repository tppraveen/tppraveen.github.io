Schlumberger - SLB
Duration: 3 Months

In this project, we transitioned from SAP MII and SAP MES to SAP e-services, optimizing complex and time-consuming custom development tasks. Utilizing SAP ME SDK and various services, including SAP Service Extension and Custom Services, we improved performance and efficiency, employing a skill set that included SAP MII, BLS, SAPUI5, SAP ME, and SDK DC features like Hook and Service.

The project initially relied on the robust capabilities of SAP MII and SAP MES for its developmental framework. As the project progressed, the custom development aspect primarily leaned towards SAP MII and SAPUI5 technologies, necessitating a strategic shift towards the utilization of SAP e-services to tackle intricate and time-intensive tasks effectively.


In the earlier stages, custom development within SAP MII extensively employed Business Logic Scripting (BLS) in conjunction with SQL queries for data manipulation and retrieval. However, to enhance the system's efficiency and manage complexity, these methodologies were supplanted by the SAP ME SDK, offering advanced capabilities for intricate logic formulation and comprehensive data retrieval processes.


Furthermore, the project's evolution saw the introduction and implementation of various services, including SAP Service Extension, Hook, and Custom Services, meticulously designed to alleviate time complexity and improve overall performance. These services played a pivotal role in optimizing the system's functionality and ensuring seamless operations.


Throughout the project lifecycle, a diverse range of skills was employed, spanning across SAP MII, BLS, SAPUI5, SAP ME, SAP ME SDK, and various SDK DC features such as Hook, Service, Web Service, and Service Extension. These competencies were instrumental in driving innovation, streamlining processes, and ultimately delivering a robust and efficient solution tailored to the project's requirements.
